NN Running Team

Eliud Kipchoge is a member of the NN Running Team, a Dutch-based professional running team founded in 2017.
The team is comprised of elite distance runners from around the world, with a focus on long-distance road and track events.
Along with Kipchoge, the team includes other renowned runners such as Kenenisa Bekele, Joshua Cheptegei, and Geoffrey Kamworor.
The NN Running Team provides support and resources to its athletes, including access to state-of-the-art training facilities
and top-level coaching, to help them achieve their goals and reach the pinnacle of the sport.

Letesenbet Gidey

Date of birth: 20 Mar 1998
Country: Ethiopia
She has won multiple gold medals at the African and World Championships, and set a new world record in the women's 5000m race in 2020.

Joshua Cheptegei

Date of birth: 12 Sep 1996
Country: Uganda
Cheptegei set a new world record in the men's 5,000m race, breaking a 16-year-old record previously held by Kenenisa Bekele.

Yalemzerf Yehualaw

Date of birth: 03 Aug 1999
Country: Ethiopia
Ethiopian long-distance runner who specializes in road races. She won the Istanbul Half Marathon with a time of 1:04:46, setting a new world record for the women's half marathon.

Kenenisa Bekele

Date of birth: 13 Jun 1982
Country: Ethiopia
Ethiopian long-distance runner, widely considered as one of the greatest distance runners of all time. Bekele has won numerous gold medals and set several world records.

Genzebe Dibaba

Date of birth: 08 Feb 1991
Country: Ethiopia
Ethiopian middle-distance runner who holds multiple world records. She registered an electric 2:18:05 to finish second in the Amsterdam Marathon.

Abdi Nageeye

Date of birth: 02 Mar 1989
Country: The Netherlands
Dutch long-distance runner who specializes in track and road events. He holds the Dutch national records in the half marathon and the marathon.

Lonah Salpeter

Date of birth: 12 Dec 1988
Country: Israel
Kenyan-born Israeli long-distance runner who competes in track and road running events. She specializes in long-distance events such as the 10,000m and the marathon.

Geoffrey Kamworor

Date of birth: 22 Nov 1992
Country: Kenya
Kenyan long-distance runner who specializes in track, and road running events. Setting a world record in the half marathon in 2019. His World Half-Marathon title in Cardiff is 59:10.
